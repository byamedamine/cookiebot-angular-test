Cookiebot Angular SPA Bug Test

This is an Angular Single-Page Application with Zone.js to reproduce the JavaScript error when clicking "Change your consent" on the Cookie Declaration page.

🐛 Bug Reproduction Steps

  1. Open this site in Incognito / Private mode.
  2. When the Cookiebot consent banner appears, click Save settings.
  3. Click Cookie Declaration in the nav — do NOT hard-refresh.
  4. Open DevTools → Console (F12).
  5. Click the "Change your consent" button at the bottom.
  6. Observe the JS error in the console.
